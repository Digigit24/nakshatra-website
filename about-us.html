Our Story

Welcome to Nakshatra IVF

Pregnancy and childbirth are milestones in a woman’s life. To make this journey pleasant, enjoyable and safe, Nakshatra clinic, one of thebest infertility clinics in Baner, Hinjewadi, Wakad, Ravet & Pune, provides complete care to new mothers and mothers-to-be. We offer treatments to couples who are facing difficulty with conceiving.

We offer the most advanced treatment, including gynecology, Obstetrics, Fertility (both men and women), Cosmetic gynecology, sexual medicine  etc. Headed by highly qualified IVF specialist Dr Ramit Kamate, we are the most reputable and best infertility clinic in Baner, Hinjewadi, Wakad, Ravet & Pune.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide ethical, patient-centered care in a comfortable and supportive environment. We are committed to offering the highest standards of fertility, gynecology, obstetrics, and sexual medicine services.

Under the leadership of Dr. Ramit Kamate, we focus on delivering individualized treatment plans, backed by cutting-edge medical technology and compassionate expertise. Our goal is to help couples achieve their dreams of parenthood while prioritizing their emotional, physical, and mental well-being.

Our Vision


At Nakshatra Clinic, our vision is to be the leading center of excellence in fertility and women’s health, providing compassionate care, advanced treatments, and personalized services. We strive to empower every couple on their journey to parenthood, ensuring a positive and fulfilling experience.

By continuously embracing the latest medical innovations, we aim to provide hope and build healthier futures for families across Baner, Hinjewadi, Wakad, Ravet, and Pune.

The Founder

Dr. Ramit Raosaheb Kamate

MBBS ,DNB, DGO, FRM (UK)

Dr. Ramit Kamate is a Reproductive Medicine Consultant and Sexologist with experience of 12+ years. He specialises in Sexual Medicine for male and female, Fertility Treatment, Pre and Post Delivery Care, Normal Vaginal Delivery (NVD), Tubectomy/Tubal Ligation, Natural Cycle IVF, MTP.

Dr. Ramit Kamate finished MBBS from B J Medical College, Pune. He pursued Masters’s In Reproductive Medicine from Hamilton University, UK & IBCME Dubai. Completed Fellowship in Cosmetic Gynaecology and Sexual Medicine from USA.
