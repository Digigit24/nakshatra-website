Abnormal Uterine Bleeding

Irregular or heavy menstrual bleeding can disrupt your physical, emotional, and reproductive health. If your periods are prolonged, unpredictable, or unusually heavy, you may be experiencing Abnormal Uterine Bleeding (AUB)—a common condition across all age groups.

At our advanced gynecology and fertility clinic in Pune, we provide complete evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment for AUB using evidence-based therapies and personalised care. Our goal is to restore hormonal balance, regulate cycles, and help you live freely without discomfort or anxiety.

Treatment Options for Abnormal Uterine Bleeding

Hormonal Therapy

Balances estrogen–progesterone to regulate cycles, reduce cramps, and control heavy bleeding (useful in PCOS & imbalance).

Tranexamic Acid & NSAIDs

Medicines that improve clotting and reduce uterine inflammation—lighter, more manageable periods with pain relief.

Oral Contraceptive Pills

Keeps hormones steady, prevents endometrial thickening, and reduces bleeding & pain—non-surgical first-line option.

Progesterone Therapy

Ideal for irregular/prolonged cycles due to low progesterone; helps regular shedding and prevents abnormal thickening.

Hormone-Releasing IUD

Thins uterine lining and offers long-term, reversible control of heavy bleeding—daily pills not required.

Hysteroscopy (Minimally Invasive)

A tiny camera treats/removes polyps or fibroids and diagnoses thickened tissue—fast recovery, precise results.

Other options based on cause & family plans include laparoscopy (for fibroids, cysts, endometriosis), endometrial ablation (for completed families), myomectomy (fibroid removal with uterus preserved), and in select severe cases, hysterectomy .
