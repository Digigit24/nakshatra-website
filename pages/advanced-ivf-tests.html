Advanced IVF Tests

Good testing builds great treatment plans. Our advanced IVF test panel gives a clear picture of ovarian reserve, sperm health, uterine environment, hormones, and genetics—so your plan is precise, not guesswork.

What We Assess (Female)

  • Ovarian Reserve: AMH (Anti-Müllerian Hormone) and AFC (Antral Follicle Count) scan
  • Hormone Profile: FSH, LH, Estradiol (E2), Progesterone, Prolactin, TSH/Thyroid panel
  • Metabolic Health: Fasting glucose, insulin, HbA1c, Vitamin D, B12 (as advised)
  • Uterine & Tubal Assessment: TVS pelvic ultrasound, SIS (Saline Infusion Sonography) / HSG (as indicated)
  • Genetic & Infection Screen: Karyotype, carrier screening (when needed), mandatory infection panel

What We Assess (Male)

  • Semen Analysis: Volume, count, motility, morphology (WHO guidelines)
  • DNA Fragmentation Index (DFI): Sperm DNA integrity for recurrent failures/miscarriages
  • Hormones (when needed): FSH, LH, Testosterone, Prolactin, TSH
  • Infection & Genetic Tests: Screening and karyotype/ Y-microdeletions (as advised)

Your Testing Pathway

1

Clinical Consultation

We review history, prior reports, and set a targeted testing plan—no unnecessary tests, only what moves the needle.

2

Female Workup

Bloods for AMH & hormones, ultrasound/AFC, and uterine evaluation (SIS/HSG) as indicated.

3

Male Workup

Advanced semen analysis with DFI where required; targeted hormonal and genetic panels if red flags appear.

4

Combine & Interpret

We stitch the data together—reserve, embryo potential, uterine readiness, sperm quality—to map the shortest path to pregnancy.

5

Personalised Plan

Supplements, timing, stimulation protocol, ICSI/PGT decisions, and add-ons—chosen because your numbers say they’ll help.

Key Advanced Tests We Offer

  • AMH & AFC: Core ovarian reserve metrics to plan dose and expectations
  • Hormone Day-2/3 Panel: FSH/LH/E2 balance for diagnosis and protocol selection
  • SIS / HSG: Detects polyps, fibroids, adhesions, and tubal patency issues
  • DFI (Sperm DNA Fragmentation): Identifies hidden sperm damage impacting embryos
  • Genetic Screening: Karyotype and carrier tests where history suggests risk
  • Infection Panel: Standard pre-ART viral/serology screening for both partners

Testing is not about doing more—it's about finding what matters for you . Our diagnostics are focused, evidence-based, and designed to improve outcomes.

How Tests Change Outcomes

Accurate testing helps pick the right stimulation, decide IVF vs ICSI, choose embryo day and number, and time transfer. For many couples, a few small tweaks—based on test results—make the big difference.
