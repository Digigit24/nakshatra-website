Bad Obstetric History (BOH) Treatment

Experiencing repeated miscarriages or stillbirths can be heartbreaking. If you’ve faced pregnancy loss in the past, it’s time to seek expert help. Our Bad Obstetric History (BOH) Treatment in Pune focuses on finding the root cause, restoring your reproductive health, and helping you achieve a healthy pregnancy.
At our fertility and maternity clinic in Pune, we combine advanced medical technology with compassionate care to provide complete evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment for women with a history of pregnancy complications.

Advanced BOH Diagnostic Tests

Hormone Profile Tests

TSH, prolactin & thyroid antibodies to detect imbalances impacting fertility and pregnancy.

TORCH Screening

Checks toxoplasmosis, rubella, CMV & herpes to prevent infection-related losses.

Karyotype Analysis

Parental chromosome study to identify genetic causes of recurrent miscarriage.

Autoimmune Profile

ANA, lupus anticoagulant & antiphospholipid antibodies for immune-related loss.

Coagulation Studies

dRVVT, cardiolipin & other factors to detect clotting disorders affecting placental flow.

Ultrasound & Hysteroscopy

Evaluates uterus, lining & structural anomalies impacting implantation.

Vitamin D & Nutrition

Vitamin D, iron & folate assessment essential for maternal health and fetal development.


BOH Treatments

1. Hormonal Therapy

Corrects thyroid/prolactin/progesterone issues to regulate cycles and support implantation.

2. Anticoagulant & Antiplatelet

Heparin/low-dose aspirin for APS & clotting issues to improve placental blood flow.

3. Infection Control

Treats TORCH/UTI/fungal infections safely before and during pregnancy.

4. Nutritional & Vitamin Support

Vitamin D, folate & iron plans to enhance fertility and fetal growth.

5. Lifestyle & Stress Management

Counselling, diet, yoga & weight care to improve hormonal balance and outcomes.

6. Preconception & Pregnancy Planning

Health optimization, monitored conception timing, scans & personalized follow-ups.

Common causes include genetic abnormalities, hormonal imbalances, infections, immune disorders, uterine problems, or lifestyle factors. Our Pune specialists perform detailed testing to find and treat the underlying cause for better pregnancy outcomes.

Treatment for BOH in Pune includes hormonal therapy, infection control, vitamin supplementation, genetic counseling, and fertility monitoring. Our expert gynecologists create personalized plans to improve conception and ensure a healthy pregnancy.
