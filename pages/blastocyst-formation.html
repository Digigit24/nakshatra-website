Blastocyst Formation

At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we offer advanced fertility treatments like blastocyst formation and transfer to help couples increase their chances of a successful pregnancy. This advanced technique allows our specialists to carefully select embryos with the best implantation potential, bringing you one step closer to your dream of parenthood. With our modern IVF lab, experienced fertility experts, and compassionate care, we create a treatment plan that’s personalised just for you.

Blastocyst Culture & Transfer: How It Works

1
Egg Retrieval & Fertilisation

Mature eggs collected and fertilised via IVF/ICSI under expert supervision.

2
Embryo Culture

Embryos grown in state-of-the-art incubators mimicking natural conditions.

3
Monitoring & Grading

Continuous checks on cell division and morphology to pick best performers.

4
Blastocyst Selection

Day 5/6 embryos selected for fresh transfer or vitrification (FET).

5
Embryo Transfer

A gentle, ultrasound-guided transfer of a single high-quality blastocyst is usually performed. This approach minimizes multiple pregnancy risks while maintaining excellent success rates..

Frequently Asked Questions

Blastocyst formation in IVF occurs when a fertilized egg develops for about five days into a mature stage called a blastocyst. This stage has more cells and better implantation potential, increasing the IVF success rate. At Nakshatra Clinic Pune, specialists monitor this growth closely to select the healthiest embryos for transfer.

Blastocyst culture allows embryos to grow longer in a controlled lab setting, helping embryologists identify the most viable ones for transfer. This improves implantation chances and reduces multiple pregnancy risks. Fertility clinics in Pune use this technique to enhance IVF outcomes and ensure healthier pregnancies.

During a day-5 blastocyst transfer, a mature blastocyst is placed directly into the uterus using a thin catheter. This precise procedure, performed at leading fertility clinics in Pune, aligns with the natural timing of embryo implantation, improving the chances of a successful pregnancy.

The cost of blastocyst culture and transfer in Pune varies depending on the IVF package and lab techniques used. At Nakshatra Clinic, the cost is transparent and affordable, ensuring patients receive advanced fertility treatment with the latest technology while maintaining high IVF success rates.

Blastocyst transfer offers multiple benefits — higher implantation rates, fewer embryos needed per transfer, and lower chances of multiple pregnancies. Patients at Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, often experience better IVF success rates and more natural embryo selection through this advanced fertility approach.

Blastocyst formation is most suitable for patients with multiple healthy embryos during IVF. It allows for natural selection of the strongest embryos for transfer. At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, fertility experts assess each case individually to determine whether blastocyst culture will improve your IVF success chances.
