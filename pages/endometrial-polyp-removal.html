Endometrial Polyp Removal

At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we understand that women’s health concerns like endometrial (uterine) polyps can be both uncomfortable and worrisome. These small, non-cancerous growths in the uterus often cause symptoms such as irregular periods, heavy bleeding, or difficulty conceiving, affecting both physical and emotional well-being. Our caring and experienced gynaecologists take the time to listen, explain, and guide you through every step of the process. Using the latest hysteroscopic and minimally invasive treatments, we focus on safely removing polyps while preserving your fertility and ensuring a smooth recovery. At Nakshatra Clinic, your comfort, confidence, and long-term reproductive health are always our top priorities.

Treatment Options for Endometrial Polyps

Watchful Monitoring (Observation Approach)

For small, symptom-free polyps, immediate surgery may not be necessary. Our experts at Nakshatra Clinic carefully monitor your uterine health through periodic ultrasounds and hormonal evaluations. This approach is ideal for women nearing menopause or with minimal symptoms, ensuring intervention only when needed while maintaining natural hormonal balance.

Medication-Based Management

Medications are often the first line of treatment for mild cases or to control bleeding before surgery. Hormonal therapies such as progestins or gonadotropin-releasing hormone (GnRH) agonists help reduce estrogen levels and shrink the polyps temporarily. However, since polyps can recur after stopping medication, our doctors use this as a short-term solution to stabilize symptoms before definitive removal.

Hysteroscopic Polypectomy (Minimally Invasive Removal)

This is the gold-standard treatment for uterine polyps at Nakshatra Clinic, Pune. Using a hysteroscope — a thin, lighted instrument with a tiny camera — our gynecologist visualizes the uterine cavity and precisely removes polyps without harming healthy tissue. Painless and scar-free Day-care procedure Rapid recovery (within 24–48 hours) Preserves fertility and uterine function

Dilation and Curettage (D&C)

In cases involving multiple or larger polyps, or when hysteroscopy is not feasible, D&C may be performed. This technique involves gently scraping the uterine lining to remove excess tissue and restore menstrual regularity. At Nakshatra Clinic, D&C is done under safe anesthesia conditions, using advanced suction technology to minimize discomfort and ensure thorough tissue removal.

Combined Treatment with Hysteroscopy and Biopsy

For women with recurring polyps, abnormal bleeding, or postmenopausal symptoms, our specialists recommend a combined diagnostic and treatment approach. During hysteroscopy, a targeted biopsy is also performed to rule out precancerous or cancerous changes. This dual-step process ensures both treatment and early detection, safeguarding your long-term reproductive health.

Fertility-Focused Polyp Removal

For women undergoing IVF or trying to conceive naturally, even small uterine polyps can interfere with embryo implantation. At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we specialize in fertility-preserving hysteroscopic removal, which enhances implantation rates and pregnancy success. Our gynecologists coordinate closely with fertility experts to ensure the best outcomes with minimal disruption to your reproductive cycle.

Post-Treatment Hormonal & Lifestyle Support

After polyp removal, our team focuses on preventing recurrence. We provide personalized hormonal therapy, dietary guidance, and lifestyle recommendations to balance estrogen levels and maintain uterine health. Regular follow-ups at Nakshatra Clinic help monitor recovery, optimize menstrual health, and support future fertility goals.
