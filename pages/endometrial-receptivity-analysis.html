Endometrial Receptivity Analysis (ERA) Test

Every woman’s body is unique, and so is her fertility journey. The Endometrial Receptivity Analysis (ERA) Test in Pune is an advanced genetic fertility test designed to identify the exact moment when the uterus is most receptive for embryo implantation. This cutting-edge ERA test for IVF in Pune helps fertility experts precisely time embryo transfer, significantly improving the success rate of IVF treatments and reducing implantation failures. At our fertility and IVF center in Pune, experienced specialists personalize every IVF plan based on ERA results ensuring a higher pregnancy rate, scientifically backed treatment, and personalized fertility care to help you achieve your dream of parenthood with confidence and care.

Testing Services Provided

1. Pre-Test Consultation

Every ERA journey begins with a detailed consultation. Our fertility expert explains the process, reviews your IVF history, and identifies whether the ERA test is suitable for your case — particularly if you’ve experienced repeated implantation failures or unexplained infertility.

2. Mock Embryo Transfer Cycle

Before your actual embryo transfer, a mock cycle is conducted. During this period, you’ll receive progesterone supplements for 4–5 days to prepare your endometrial lining. This mimics the hormonal conditions of a real IVF cycle.

3. Endometrial Biopsy Collection

A small sample of your endometrial tissue is collected using a simple, outpatient procedure. The biopsy is painless, quick, and safe. This sample is then sent to a specialized genetic lab for detailed molecular analysis..

4. Genetic & Molecular Analysis

In the lab, the gene expression profile of your uterine lining is analyzed. This helps determine whether your endometrium is receptive, pre-receptive, or post-receptive, guiding the timing of your embryo transfer with precision..

5. Personalized Embryo Transfer Plan

Based on the ERA report, your fertility specialist will create a personalized embryo transfer plan aligned with your unique implantation window. This tailored approach enhances the likelihood of implantation and overall IVF success.

6. Post-Test Review & Follow-Up

After the results, our team provides a detailed report explanation and adjusts your IVF or Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET) schedule accordingly. Ongoing support, emotional counseling, and fertility care continue throughout your journey.
