Follicular Study

Conceiving naturally or with the help of fertility treatments begins with understanding your body’s natural rhythm, especially when ovulation occurs. At Nakshatra Clinic, Balewadi, Pune, we offer follicular study, a simple ultrasound-based test that gives you a clear picture of your ovulation cycle, egg growth, and uterine health. If you’ve been trying to conceive for a while or are preparing for an IUI or IVF treatment, a follicular study in Pune can make a real difference. By closely tracking your ovulation, our fertility experts help you pinpoint the best time to try for pregnancy, identify any hormonal or ovulatory issues early, and increase your chances of success—naturally or with medical support.

Step-by-Step Process of a Follicular Study

1. Baseline Scan (Day 2–3)

Your first scan checks for baseline follicle size, uterine lining, and ovarian health. Any cysts or abnormalities are noted before proceeding.

2. Serial Scans (Every 2–3 Days)

Over the next few days, the doctor conducts transvaginal ultrasounds to track follicle development. Each follicle grows approximately 2 mm per day. As it matures, hormone levels (especially estrogen) increase, thickening the uterine lining..

3. Ovulation Detection

When the dominant follicle reaches 18–25 mm in diameter, ovulation is expected within 24–36 hours.

At this stage, your doctor will advise:

  • Timed intercourse for natural conception, or
  • IUI/IVF egg retrieval at the optimal moment.

4. Endometrial Assessment

The thickness and pattern of your uterine lining are checked. A healthy endometrium should measure 8–10 mm and appear trilaminar (three-layered), indicating readiness for implantation.

5. Final Confirmation Scan

A follow-up scan confirms whether the follicle has ruptured, indicating successful ovulation. Based on this, further treatment or monitoring is advised. Each scan takes around 15–20 minutes, and the number of visits varies depending on your cycle and ovulation pattern.

Frequently Asked Questions

A Follicular Study, also called Follicular Monitoring or Ovulation Tracking, is a painless transvaginal ultrasound procedure performed several times during a woman’s menstrual cycle. The purpose is to observe the growth and maturity of ovarian follicles — tiny fluid-filled sacs that contain eggs. Each follicle develops and grows in response to hormones, particularly FSH (Follicle Stimulating Hormone) and LH (Luteinizing Hormone). By measuring their size and number during your cycle, our fertility specialists can predict the exact time of ovulation and determine your most fertile days.
