High-Risk Pregnancy

Pregnancy is one of the most beautiful phases of a woman’s life. However, not every pregnancy follows a smooth journey — some require specialised medical attention and close monitoring. High-risk pregnancy treatment in Pune is essential for expecting mothers who face complications such as hypertension, diabetes, thyroid imbalance, or previous pregnancy losses. With expert care, timely diagnosis, and personalised medical support, even high-risk pregnancies can result in safe deliveries and healthy babies.
At our advanced maternity and obstetric care clinic in Pune, our experienced gynaecologists and maternal-foetal medicine specialists provide comprehensive care, ensuring the safety and well-being of both mother and baby throughout the pregnancy journey.


Pregnancy-Related Complications Leading to High-Risk Pregnancy

Premature Labor

Labor before 37 weeks; early care reduces breathing issues and low birth weight risks.

Placenta Previa

Placenta covering the cervix; requires monitoring and a guided, safe birth plan.

Fetal Growth Limits/Anomalies

More likely with twins/triplets; scans and genetics aid early detection.


High-Risk Pregnancy Care at Our Pune Clinic

Advanced Tests & Monitoring

Regular ultrasounds, blood tests, and fetal assessments to track growth and risks.

Diet & Lifestyle Plans

Custom nutrition and activity to manage diabetes, hypertension, and thyroid issues.

24/7 Obstetric Support

Round-the-clock assistance and emergency response for complications.

Emotional Counseling

Guidance to manage stress and anxiety common in high-risk pregnancies.

Safe Delivery Planning

Normal or C-section plans tailored for the safest outcome for mother and baby.


Prevention & Management of High-Risk Pregnancy

Pre-Pregnancy Evaluation: Screen diabetes, thyroid, and hormonal health to plan a safer conception.

Timely Immunizations: Tetanus, flu and other vaccines to protect mother and baby.

Healthy Weight & Nutrition: Protein- and micronutrient-rich plans to prevent GD, high BP and low birth weight.

Controlled Activity: Doctor-approved exercise to improve circulation and reduce stress safely.

Manage Chronic Conditions: Ongoing monitoring and medicines for diabetes, thyroid and hypertension.

Regular ANC & Ultrasounds: Advanced imaging and tests to track fetal growth and intervene early.


Specialists Involved in High-Risk Care

Obstetrician/Gynecologist (OBGYN)

Leads trimester-wise monitoring, testing and delivery planning for mother and baby.

Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM)

Manages complex cases—hypertension, diabetes, thyroid, twins—with advanced surveillance.

Diet & Lifestyle Experts

Personalized nutrition and safe activity plans to support maternal and fetal health.

Counseling & Emotional Support

Stress management, coping strategies and confidence-building throughout pregnancy.
