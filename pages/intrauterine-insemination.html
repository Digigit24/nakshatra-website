Intrauterine Insemination

Every fertility journey is unique, and finding the right support can make all the difference. Our IUI treatment in Pune offers a safe, effective, and affordable fertility solution for couples struggling to conceive naturally. IUI (Intrauterine Insemination) is a simple assisted reproductive technique that increases the chances of fertilization by placing healthy sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation. Our fertility clinic provides personalized care, advanced diagnostic facilities, and compassionate support from consultation to conception. Whether you’re facing unexplained infertility, mild male factor issues, or ovulation problems, our fertility specialists in Pune are here to guide you toward your dream of parenthood.

IUI Treatment Process at Nakshatra Clinic

1. Fertility Evaluation & Counselling

Our specialists begin with a thorough fertility check-up, including hormone testing, ovulation tracking, and semen analysis. This helps identify the cause of infertility and create a personalised IUI treatment plan in Pune that suits your specific health and reproductive goals..

2. Ovulation Induction & Monitoring

Using safe and mild fertility medications, ovulation is stimulated to improve egg quality and timing. Our fertility experts in Pune monitor follicle growth through ultrasound scans and hormone levels to ensure insemination is performed at the most fertile time.

3. Sperm Preparation

In our advanced fertility lab, the sperm sample is processed through “washing,” which separates healthy and motile sperm from other components. This step ensures that only the best sperm are used during intrauterine insemination (IUI), enhancing fertilisation chances and pregnancy success.

4. IUI Procedure

During the IUI treatment, the prepared sperm is carefully inserted into the uterus using a fine catheter. The procedure is quick, painless, and takes only a few minutes. At our Pune clinic, it’s done under expert supervision in a calm and sterile environment.

5. Post-Procedure Care

After insemination, patients can resume normal activities. Our fertility specialists may prescribe supportive medications and recommend a pregnancy test after 14 days. At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we guide you through every stage with medical and emotional support..

6. Donor IUI Services

For couples facing severe male infertility or genetic issues, we provide donor sperm IUI treatment in Pune. All donors undergo strict medical screening for safety and compatibility, ensuring ethical and confidential fertility treatment for hopeful parents.
