IVF Options

Infertility is a common concern among couples of various ages. This challenge often leads to emotional stress and lowered confidence. However, at Nakshatra Clinic, one of the best IVF clinics in Pune, we believe that parenthood is a dream worth pursuing—and with the right medical support, it is absolutely achievable. There may be several reasons for infertility in men and women, and not all of them are naturally reversible. Hence, IVF treatment in Pune is often an ideal solution for a wide range of infertility-related issues such as blocked fallopian tubes, low sperm count, ovulation disorders, or unexplained infertility. The full form of IVF is *In Vitro Fertilisation*—a carefully structured process designed to help individuals or couples conceive, prevent genetic issues, and achieve healthy parenthood. At Nakshatra Clinic, we provide advanced IVF options in Pune using modern reproductive technologies, personalized treatment plans, and compassionate care to guide you every step of the way. Our goal is to offer affordable IVF services without compromising on quality, ensuring every couple has a fair chance to experience the joy of parenthood.

IVF Treatment Options at Nakshatra Clinic

ICSI - Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection

ICSI is an advanced IVF procedure in Pune for male infertility. A single sperm is injected directly into an egg to boost fertilisation chances. This method is ideal for men with low sperm count or motility and offers excellent success rates under expert embryologist supervision.

Donor Egg IVF

For couples who cannot use their own eggs or sperm, donor IVF treatments provide an effective alternative. All donors at our Pune fertility centre are thoroughly screened for health and genetics. This process helps couples achieve pregnancy safely and ethically.

Frozen Embryo Transfer

Frozen Embryo Transfer allows the use of preserved embryos from previous IVF cycles. It provides better timing and flexibility while increasing implantation success. Our Pune IVF specialists ensure optimal uterine preparation for the best results in each transfer cycle..

Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD)

PGT helps identify chromosomal or genetic abnormalities in embryos before transfer, increasing IVF success and reducing miscarriage risk. Our IVF centre in Pune recommends this for couples with repeated IVF failures or known genetic disorders.

Blastocyst Culture and Transfer

This technique involves growing embryos for 5–6 days until they reach the blastocyst stage. Only the healthiest embryos are selected for transfer, improving pregnancy chances. Our IVF lab in Pune uses advanced technology to ensure precise embryo development and selection.

ICM - Inner Cell Mass

ICM technology helps identify the best embryos based on their cellular quality, ensuring that the most viable embryos are chosen for implantation.
