Male infertility treatment...

Are you and your partner struggling to conceive? You’re not alone—male infertility is more common than most people realise, and the good news is that it’s treatable.
At the Nakshatra IVF & Sexual Wellness Clinic in Pune, we specialise in helping men improve their fertility through personalised treatment, advanced technology, and compassionate care.
Our approach is simple — we listen, we understand, and we treat the root cause, not just the symptoms. Whether it’s hormonal imbalance, poor sperm health, or lifestyle factors, our team is here to help you every step of the way.

Common Male Infertility Conditions We Treat

Azoospermia (No Sperm in Semen)

This condition means sperm are absent in the ejaculate. We use advanced treatments like TESA, PESA, and Micro-TESE to retrieve sperm directly from the testes, giving couples a real chance at conception through IVF or ICSI.

DNA Fragmentation

When sperm DNA is damaged, it can reduce the chances of successful fertilisation and pregnancy. We perform DNA Fragmentation Index (DFI) tests and offer antioxidant therapy and lifestyle modifications to restore sperm health.

Oligospermia (Low Sperm Count)

Low sperm count doesn’t mean no hope. With hormonal therapy, dietary plans, and targeted medications , we help improve sperm production naturally and safely.

Oligo-Astheno-Teratozoospermia (OAT)

This involves low count, poor movement, and abnormal shape of sperm. We design custom fertility plans , combining IVF, ICSI, and lifestyle support to enhance conception success rates.

Our Male Infertility Treatments

Semen Analysis & Hormone Testing

Comprehensive semen and hormone testing helps identify sperm health, count, and hormonal imbalances to guide the most effective fertility treatment plan.

Genetic & Scrotal Ultrasound Testing

Advanced imaging and genetic screening detect structural or hereditary issues affecting sperm production, helping design precise, targeted infertility treatments.

Varicocele Treatment

We provide surgical and non-surgical solutions for varicocele to improve blood flow, sperm quality, and natural fertility outcomes in men.

Sperm Retrieval Techniques (TESA, PESA, Micro-TESE)

For men with azoospermia, our advanced sperm retrieval procedures extract healthy sperm directly for use in IVF or ICSI cycles.

Lifestyle & Nutritional Counseling

Personalized diet, exercise, and stress management plans enhance sperm quality and overall reproductive health naturally and effectively.

Hormonal & Medical Therapy

Targeted medications and hormonal treatments restore balance, boost testosterone, and support natural sperm production and reproductive function.

Antioxidant & DNA Fragmentation Therapy

Our antioxidant therapy reduces sperm DNA damage, improving fertilization potential and embryo quality for better pregnancy outcomes.

Continuous Monitoring & Follow-up

Regular follow-ups ensure progress tracking, timely adjustments in treatment, and long-term improvement in male fertility outcomes.
