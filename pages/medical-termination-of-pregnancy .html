Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP)

Facing an unplanned pregnancy can be emotionally overwhelming, but you don't have to navigate it alone. At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we provide safe, legal, confidential, and medically supervised Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP) following all guidelines under the Indian MTP Act. Our expert gynaecologists ensure a judgement-free, supportive, and respectful environment where you can make informed decisions about your reproductive health. Whether you need medical guidance, emotional support, or clarity about the process, our Pune clinic is committed to offering personalised, ethical, and compassionate care.

Our MTP Services

Comprehensive Pre-Procedure Consultation

At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we begin with a detailed consultation to assess your medical history, gestational age, and overall health. Our gynecologists explain the entire MTP process, safety precautions, expected symptoms, and emotional support options, ensuring clarity and comfort before starting the procedure.

Ultrasound & Diagnostic Screening

We perform an advanced ultrasound to confirm pregnancy, rule out ectopic complications, and determine the safe timeline for MTP. Nakshatra Clinic’s Pune facility uses accurate imaging to ensure maximum safety. Diagnostic tests help personalize treatment and reduce risks, offering complete medical confidence before medication.

Medication-Based Pregnancy Termination

Nakshatra Clinic provides medically supervised, legally compliant MTP using approved medications. We guide you through dosage, administration, rest, and care instructions. Our Pune team remains available throughout the process to ensure safety, manage symptoms, and make the experience as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Pain, Bleeding & Symptom Management

During MTP, you may experience bleeding and cramps similar to a heavy period. At our Pune clinic, we provide safe pain-relief options, hydration advice, and detailed management tips. Our doctors help you understand normal versus concerning symptoms, ensuring comfort and confidence throughout recovery.

Post-MTP Scan & Follow-Up Care

After the medication phase, we conduct a follow-up ultrasound at Nakshatra Clinic, Pune to ensure complete termination and healthy recovery. Our team monitors bleeding patterns, uterus health, and hormone balance, providing any necessary treatments and emotional support for a safe return to normal routines.

Emotional Support & Confidential Care

MTP can be emotionally difficult, and we’re here to support you with compassion and confidentiality. Nakshatra Clinic, Pune ensures your privacy is protected at every step. Our team offers a non-judgmental environment, emotional counselling, and trustworthy guidance to help you feel respected and understood.

Future Fertility, Health & Contraception Guidance

Our Pune gynecologists offer personalized fertility and contraception counselling after MTP. We guide you on safe birth control options, menstrual health, and planning future pregnancies. Nakshatra Clinic ensures your reproductive wellbeing is protected long-term, helping you make informed decisions about your health and family planning.
