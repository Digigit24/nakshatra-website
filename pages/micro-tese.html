Micro-TESE Treatment

Microsurgical Testicular Sperm Extraction (Micro-TESE) is an advanced surgical procedure designed to retrieve sperm directly from the male testicles. It is primarily recommended for men diagnosed with Non-Obstructive Azoospermia (NOA), a condition where sperm is absent in the ejaculate, which is a common cause of male infertility. At Nakshatra Clinic in Pune, our experienced urologists and IVF specialists perform micro-TESE using high-precision microscopic technology, ensuring the highest chance of sperm retrieval with minimal damage to the testicular tissue.

Our Micro-TESE Services

Expert Urologists & IVF Specialists

Our experienced doctors perform Micro-TESE with high precision, ensuring minimal tissue damage. Each procedure is personalized, enhancing sperm retrieval success and providing comprehensive guidance for fertility treatments such as IVF or ICSI.

Microsurgical Sperm Retrieval

Using a high-power operating microscope, our specialists examine testicular tissue to locate sperm-rich tubules. This microsurgical approach improves the chance of sperm retrieval while protecting healthy testicular tissue.

Safe, Minimally Invasive

Micro-TESE involves a small incision in the scrotum, performed under anesthesia. The technique ensures minimal risk, fast recovery, and reduced discomfort, making it a safe option for men facing severe infertility challenges.

Advanced Laboratory Analysis

Sperm tissue retrieved during Micro-TESE is carefully analyzed by expert andrology technologists. This ensures viable sperm are isolated and preserved for fertilization through ICSI or IVF, maximizing the chances of conception.

Multi-Layer Closure & Recovery

Our multi-layer closure technique allows the surgical incision to heal naturally with dissolvable stitches. This reduces the risk of bleeding or infection and promotes rapid recovery for patients post-surgery.

Pain Management & Follow-Up

We provide tailored post-surgical care, including pain management with safe medications and guidance on activity restriction. Our team monitors patients closely to ensure smooth recovery and optimal fertility outcomes.

High Success Rates

Micro-TESE performed by skilled specialists significantly increases sperm retrieval success. The precision of the procedure, combined with lab expertise, ensures that even severe male infertility cases have improved chances of successful conception.

Alternative Options

If Micro-TESE is not suitable, our team guides patients on alternatives such as sperm donation or adoption, providing comprehensive fertility counseling to help couples make informed reproductive choices.
