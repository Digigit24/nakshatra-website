Common Women’s Health Conditions We Treat

Our specialists have extensive experience in diagnosing and managing common and complex gynecological issues using advanced technology and minimally invasive procedures.

When menstrual bleeding becomes unusually heavy, prolonged, or irregular, it may indicate hormonal imbalance or uterine conditions. Our experts use advanced diagnostic tools and treatments — including hormonal therapy and hysteroscopy — to restore normal menstrual health.

Fibroids are non-cancerous growths in the uterus that may cause abdominal pain, heavy periods, or pressure symptoms. We offer both medical management and laparoscopic surgery to remove fibroids safely and preserve fertility.

This minimally invasive surgical technique uses a small camera to diagnose and treat pelvic conditions like fibroids, ovarian cysts, and endometriosis. It reduces pain, recovery time, and scarring compared to traditional surgery.

A lighted instrument is inserted into the uterus to diagnose and treat problems such as fibroids, polyps, and abnormal bleeding — without the need for large incisions. This outpatient procedure offers quick recovery and precise results.

UTIs are common among women and cause burning, pain, and frequent urination. Our gynecologists provide prompt diagnosis and antibiotic treatment to prevent complications and recurrence.

Vaginal yeast or bacterial infections, often caused by Candida, can cause discomfort, discharge, and itching. We provide targeted treatments and hygiene guidance to restore balance and prevent future infections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Regular gynecological check-ups help detect early signs of uterine fibroids, hormonal imbalances, infections, or cervical abnormalities. At Nakshatra Clinic, our specialists ensure preventive screening, early diagnosis, and personalized treatment for long-term reproductive and hormonal health in women of all ages.

It’s recommended that women aged 21–65 get a Pap smear every three years to screen for cervical cancer. If you have risk factors or a family history of reproductive issues, your gynecologist in Pune may recommend more frequent tests to ensure early detection.

Abnormal Uterine Bleeding (AUB) may result from hormonal imbalance, fibroids, thyroid disorders, or stress. At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, we use advanced diagnostics like ultrasound, hormonal therapy, and hysteroscopy to identify the cause and restore a healthy menstrual cycle.

Hysteroscopy is a minimally invasive procedure that allows doctors to view and treat uterine problems like fibroids, polyps, or abnormal bleeding. At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, it helps diagnose internal issues without major surgery, ensuring quick recovery and accurate results.

A high-risk pregnancy occurs when complications like diabetes, high blood pressure, thyroid disorders, or multiple pregnancies are present. Our obstetricians in Pune provide continuous monitoring, advanced diagnostics, and personalized care to ensure a safe pregnancy and healthy delivery for both mother and baby.

To prevent infections, maintain good hygiene, drink plenty of water, and wear breathable cotton underwear. Avoid harsh soaps or douching. If you experience itching, discharge, or burning, visit Nakshatra Clinic, Pune, for accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment by our gynecologists.

You should consult a gynecologist if you experience irregular periods, pelvic pain, heavy bleeding, vaginal infections, or difficulty conceiving. Regular visits are also essential for preventive care, especially during puberty, pregnancy, and menopause, ensuring early detection and timely treatment of women’s health concerns.
