Ovarian Cysts Removal

Ovarian cysts are a common concern among women of reproductive age, often causing anxiety, discomfort, and fertility challenges. While most ovarian cysts are harmless and disappear naturally, some may grow, rupture, or twist—leading to pain and complications that need medical or surgical attention. At our advanced gynaecology and fertility clinic in Pune, we specialise in laparoscopic ovarian cyst removal using minimally invasive techniques. Our experienced gynaecologists ensure accurate diagnosis, personalised care, and safe treatment that preserves your fertility and supports overall reproductive health.

Types of Ovarian Cysts

Functional Cysts

Functional cysts are the most common type and usually harmless. They form as part of the natural menstrual cycle and often disappear on their own. These cysts are rarely painful but may cause mild bloating or pelvic discomfort. Regular monitoring through pelvic ultrasounds ensures early detection and management.

Follicular Cyst

A follicular cyst develops when a follicle does not release an egg during ovulation. Instead, it continues to grow, filling with fluid. Most follicular cysts resolve naturally within a few menstrual cycles. If persistent, your gynecologist in Pune may recommend hormonal treatment or a follow-up ultrasound.

Corpus Luteum Cyst

After ovulation, the follicle transforms into the corpus luteum, which may fill with fluid or blood, forming a cyst. These cysts can cause pelvic pain or delayed periods. They usually disappear naturally, but larger ones may require medical observation or minimal surgical removal if symptoms worsen.

Endometrioma (Chocolate Cyst)

Endometriomas occur due to endometriosis, a condition where uterine lining tissue grows outside the uterus and attaches to the ovary. These cysts are filled with dark, old blood, resembling chocolate. They often cause severe menstrual pain, infertility, and pelvic discomfort. Laparoscopic surgery in Pune helps effectively treat and remove them.

Dermoid Cysts (Mature Cystic Teratoma)

Dermoid cysts are benign growths that may contain tissues like hair, fat, or teeth. They develop from embryonic cells and can grow quite large, sometimes causing ovarian torsion. Though non-cancerous, they often require laparoscopic removal by an experienced gynecologist to prevent complications and preserve fertility.

Cystadenomas

Cystadenomas form on the surface of the ovary and are filled with watery or mucous-like fluid. They can grow significantly large, causing abdominal pressure, bloating, or discomfort. While typically non-cancerous, surgical removal is recommended if the cyst increases in size or causes persistent pain.

Polycystic Ovaries (PCOS-Related Cysts)

In women with Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), multiple small cysts develop on the ovaries due to hormonal imbalance. This condition can cause irregular periods, acne, weight gain, and fertility issues. Treatment focuses on hormone regulation, lifestyle changes, and fertility care available at advanced women's health clinics in Pune.

Treatment Options for Ovarian Cysts

Watchful Waiting

For small, simple ovarian cysts, doctors may recommend watchful waiting. Regular ultrasound check-ups monitor the cyst’s size and growth. Many cysts dissolve naturally without intervention. This safe, non-invasive approach is ideal for women with minimal symptoms and ensures the ovary remains healthy for future fertility.

Medication and Hormonal Therapy

Hormonal treatments such as birth control pills help regulate menstrual cycles and prevent new cysts from forming. Pain relief medications may also be prescribed. This approach effectively manages functional cysts, reduces hormonal imbalance, and helps restore reproductive health without surgery when monitored by a qualified gynecologist in Pune.

Laparoscopic Ovarian Cyst Removal

Laparoscopic ovarian cystectomy is the gold-standard surgical method for large or complex cysts. Performed through tiny incisions, it allows precise cyst removal while preserving ovarian tissue. This minimally invasive surgery ensures faster recovery, less pain, minimal scarring, and improved fertility outcomes — ideal for women seeking safe cyst removal in Pune.

Oophorectomy (Ovary Removal)

In rare cases where cysts are large, recurrent, or suspected to be cancerous, an oophorectomy may be advised. The surgeon removes the affected ovary while preserving the healthy one to maintain hormonal balance. Performed with advanced laparoscopic techniques, this procedure ensures long-term safety and effective symptom relief for patients in Pune.
