Ovulation Induction

Are you struggling to conceive due to irregular periods or ovulation problems? You’re not alone. Many women face difficulties in releasing eggs regularly, which makes conception challenging. At Nakshatra Clinic, we specialise in ovulation induction treatment in Pune, a scientifically proven fertility therapy that helps stimulate natural ovulation and improve your chances of pregnancy. Our expert fertility specialists use advanced techniques and personalised hormonal protocols to restore your body's natural rhythm and guide you toward parenthood.

Step-by-Step Ovulation Induction Procedure

1. Initial Assessment

Our fertility expert performs detailed hormone tests, ultrasound scans, and a complete reproductive health evaluation to identify the exact cause of ovulation irregularities and customize your treatment plan accordingly.

2. Medication & Stimulation

You’ll begin a prescribed course of oral or injectable fertility medications that stimulate your ovaries to produce healthy, mature eggs, carefully monitored to ensure balanced hormone levels and safe follicle growth.

3. Monitoring Phase

Throughout the stimulation phase, regular ultrasound scans and blood hormone tests are performed to track follicle development and determine the right time to trigger ovulation for the highest success rate.

4. Ovulation Trigger

When your follicles reach the ideal size, an hCG “trigger” injection is administered to release mature eggs. This step helps synchronize ovulation timing for planned intercourse or intrauterine insemination (IUI)..

5. Timed Intercourse or IUI

Depending on your fertility needs, the doctor recommends either timed intercourse or IUI. In IUI, processed sperm is placed directly into the uterus to maximize fertilization chances during the ovulation window..

6. Follow-up & Support

We provide ongoing support, follow-up consultations, and pregnancy testing to ensure your journey to parenthood is successful and well-supported.

Benefits of Ovulation Induction Treatment

Restores Natural Ovulation Cycles

Ovulation induction helps stimulate regular egg release, especially in women with irregular periods, PCOS, or hormonal imbalances. This improves your chances of conception each cycle.

Increases Pregnancy Chances

By controlling and monitoring ovulation, fertility specialists can identify your most fertile days — significantly increasing your chances of becoming pregnant naturally or through IUI.

Non-Invasive & Affordable

Compared to IVF, ovulation induction is a simple, non-surgical, and cost-effective fertility treatment that delivers great results for many couples in Pune and PCMC.

Personalized Hormonal Stimulation

Each treatment plan is customized to your body’s hormonal needs, ensuring optimal egg development and minimal side effects.

Supports Other Fertility Procedures

Ovulation induction is often the first step in fertility treatments like IUI or IVF, helping prepare your body for successful conception..

Safe & Scientifically Proven

Under the guidance of our fertility experts in Pune, ovulation induction is carefully monitored through ultrasound and hormone testing, ensuring safety and best outcomes.

Improves Egg Quality and Uterine Lining

Fertility medications enhance follicular growth and endometrial development, increasing the likelihood of implantation and a healthy pregnancy.
