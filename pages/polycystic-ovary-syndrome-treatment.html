PCOS treatment...

Are you experiencing irregular periods, unwanted hair growth, acne, or difficulty conceiving? These could be signs of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), a common hormonal disorder affecting women’s ovarian function, fertility, and overall health. At Nakshatra Clinic in Pune, we provide personalised PCOS treatments designed to help women restore hormonal balance, regulate menstrual cycles, improve fertility, and manage PCOS symptoms effectively.

PCOS Treatment Options

Lifestyle & Diet Management

A low-carbohydrate, nutrient-rich diet combined with regular exercise helps improve insulin sensitivity, regulate hormones, support ovulation, and reduce symptoms like irregular periods, excess hair growth, and acne.

Medications to Regulate Menstrual Cycles

Combination birth control pills and progestin therapy help restore regular periods, lower excess hair growth, control acne, and protect the uterine lining from long-term complications such as endometrial cancer.

Fertility-Supporting Medications

Clomiphene, Letrozole, and Metformin stimulate ovulation, improve insulin resistance, regulate blood sugar, and increase pregnancy chances by addressing underlying metabolic issues in PCOS.

Treatments for Excess Hair & Acne

Spironolactone, eflornithine cream, and electrolysis help control unwanted hair growth and acne, blocking excess androgen effects and offering long-term cosmetic relief for women with PCOS.

Hormonal Therapy & Monitoring

Targeted hormonal therapy helps correct imbalances in testosterone, LH, and SHBG, improving ovulation and fertility, while regular monitoring ensures the treatment is tailored to each woman’s specific hormonal profile.

Insulin Resistance Management

Lifestyle changes, metformin, and dietary adjustments help regulate insulin levels, improve ovarian function, support weight management, and reduce the risk of diabetes in women with PCOS.

Advanced Fertility Support

Ovulation induction, timed intercourse guidance, and IVF provide advanced fertility options for women with PCOS, maximizing pregnancy chances while addressing complex hormonal and ovarian issues.

Holistic & Personalized Care

Nakshatra Clinic offers customized PCOS plans that combine lifestyle changes, medication, fertility support, and emotional guidance to ensure long-term health, symptom control, and better fertility outcomes for every woman.
