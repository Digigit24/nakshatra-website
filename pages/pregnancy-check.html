Pregnancy Check

Pregnancy is one of the most beautiful journeys in a woman’s life, filled with joy, anticipation, and care. To make this journey smooth and worry-free, regular pregnancy checkups are essential.
At Nakshatra Clinic, a trusted pregnancy checkup clinic in Pune, we provide complete pregnancy checkups and antenatal care services under the supervision of experienced gynaecologists and obstetricians (OBGYNs). Our goal is to ensure the well-being of both mother and baby at every stage of pregnancy through expert medical guidance and compassionate care.


Our Pregnancy Checkup Services Include

Pregnancy Confirmation

Tests & ultrasound, due date, early guidance on nutrition, supplements, and lifestyle.

Regular Antenatal Visits

Track vitals, symptoms, and fetal growth across all trimesters.

Ultrasound & Growth

Scans to monitor heartbeat, position, and development, with timely care if needed.

BP, Sugar & Urine Tests

Detect gestational diabetes, infections, or hypertension early for safer pregnancy.

Nutrition & Lifestyle

Balanced diet plans, safe exercise, and supplements to support mother & baby.

High-Risk Care

Specialist monitoring for diabetes, thyroid, hypertension, or twin pregnancy.

Labor & Delivery Plan

Education, pain relief options, and delivery planning for a confident birth.

Postnatal Care

Recovery checks, breastfeeding guidance, and emotional support after delivery.


Why Are Regular Pregnancy Checkups Important?

Healthy Baby Development: Ensures your baby grows properly till full term.

Early Risk Detection: Spots diabetes, infections, or high blood pressure early.

Personalized Guidance: Every pregnancy is different; care is tailored to you.

Confidential Consultation: Discuss concerns freely—your information stays private.

Better Delivery Prep: Ongoing care prepares you for labor and birth.


Doctors Providing Pregnancy Checkups

Obstetrician/Gynecologist (OBGYN)

Complete pregnancy care from early checkups to safe delivery with trimester-wise monitoring, tests, and ultrasounds.

Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Specialist

Advanced care for high-risk or complex pregnancies—hypertension, diabetes, thyroid, twins—with continuous monitoring.

Nutrition & Lifestyle Experts

Tailored diet plans, safe workouts, and daily-routine guidance to support healthy maternal and fetal outcomes.

Counseling & Emotional Support

Coping strategies, stress support, and confidence-building for a positive pregnancy journey.
