Repeated Implantation Failure (RIF) Treatment

Going through IVF cycles in Pune can be an emotional rollercoaster, especially when your embryos fail to implant successfully. This condition is known as Repeated Implantation Failure (RIF) . RIF is typically diagnosed when two to three high-quality embryos fail to implant in consecutive IVF attempts .

While the term may sound alarming, it’s important to know that RIF doesn’t mean you cannot conceive . With an accurate diagnosis, advanced fertility treatments, and personalised care, women in Pune with RIF can still achieve pregnancy successfully.

At the Nakshatra Clinic in Pune , we focus on understanding your individual fertility profile, addressing underlying issues, and offering treatments that maximise your chances of implantation and a healthy pregnancy.

Personalised RIF Treatments

Uterine Treatments

We evaluate your uterus using hysteroscopy and imaging to detect abnormalities, scarring, or inflammation. Treatments include endometrial sampling, chronic endometritis management, and therapies like Atosiban or copper IUD placement to improve your uterus’s receptivity for embryo implantation.

Laboratory & Procedural Techniques

Advanced IVF techniques enhance implantation chances. These include assisted hatching, blastocyst-stage embryo transfer, sequential embryo transfer, IMSI, and preimplantation genetic testing (PGT-A) to select chromosomally healthy embryos for higher IVF success rates.

Imunomodulatory Therapies

For immune-related implantation failures, therapies like IVIG, intrauterine PBMC infusion, tacrolimus, G-CSF, autologous PRP, or low-dose medications (LMWH, aspirin, prednisolone) help regulate the immune response and improve the chances of embryo acceptance.

Enhancing Endometrial Receptivity

Treatments focus on optimizing the endometrium for embryo implantation. Techniques include Endometrial Receptivity Array (ERA) testing, growth hormone therapy, vaginal sildenafil, and tailoring the Window of Implantation (WOI) to improve implantation rates and overall IVF success.

Personalized Treatment Planning

Every woman’s fertility journey is unique. Our specialists design a customized RIF treatment plan based on embryo quality, uterine health, immune factors, and hormonal balance to maximize implantation success while providing ongoing monitoring, guidance, and emotional support.

RIF can result from multiple factors, including poor embryo quality, chromosomal abnormalities, uterine issues like fibroids or scarring, hormonal imbalances, or immune-related problems. Identifying the root cause helps doctors create a personalized treatment plan to improve implantation chance.

Immunotherapy helps regulate the immune system to prevent rejection of embryos. Treatments such as IVIG, intrauterine PBMC infusion, PRP therapy, or tacrolimus enhance the uterine environment, increasing the chances of embryo acceptance and successful implantation.

Most RIF treatments, including IVF procedures, hysteroscopy, and immunotherapies , are minimally invasive or performed with sedation. Discomfort is usually mild, and specialists provide guidance and support to ensure patient comfort throughout the process.

A receptive endometrium is critical for embryo implantation. Treatments like Endometrial Receptivity Array (ERA) testing, growth hormone therapy, or WOI optimization ensure the uterus is ready, improving the likelihood of a successful pregnancy in women experiencing RIF.
