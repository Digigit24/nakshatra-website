Urinary Tract Infection

Struggling with urinary tract infections in Pune? Nakshatra Clinic provides comprehensive UTI treatment for women and men—focused on accurate diagnosis, effective medicines, and long-term prevention. Our doctors assess infections of the bladder, urethra, and kidneys with urine tests, culture, imaging, and bacterial identification. Along with targeted antibiotics, we guide you on hydration, diet, and hygiene to cut recurrences. From first-time to recurrent UTI, you get compassionate care, precise treatment, and ongoing support.

Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Treatment

Detailed Consultation & Assessment

We review symptoms, history, and risks to define the UTI type and severity—so your plan is personalised for fast relief and fewer recurrences.

Laboratory Diagnosis

Urine analysis & culture identify the exact bacteria and guide targeted antibiotics—speeding recovery and preventing repeat infections.

Imaging Tests (If Required)

For recurrent/complex UTIs, ultrasound, CT/MRI, or cystoscopy help detect structural issues and plan durable treatment.

Medication & Antibiotic Therapy

Right-dose antibiotics for simple UTIs; prophylaxis when chronic. Pain relief included for burning and urgency.

Vaginal Therapy (Postmenopausal)

For low-estrogen related UTIs, local therapy restores flora & lining—cutting infection risk.

Lifestyle & Preventive Guidance

Hydration, hygiene, diet, and cranberry products (if suitable) to prevent recurrence—clear, practical steps.

Follow-Up & Monitoring: We schedule reviews to confirm cure and watch for early relapse—so you stay symptom-free long term.
