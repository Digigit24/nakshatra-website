Uterine Fibroids

Uterine fibroids (myomas) are noncancerous growths in or around the uterus. They can cause heavy bleeding, pelvic pain, bloating, or pressure symptoms—yet many women only discover them late.

At our advanced gynecology and fertility clinic in Pune, we provide complete evaluation, accurate imaging, and personalised treatment for fibroids. Whether your goal is symptom relief, fertility preservation, or definitive cure, we create a plan that fits your life.

Treatment Options for Uterine Fibroids

Medication Management

GnRH agonists to shrink fibroids, hormone IUDs to control bleeding, and NSAIDs for pain—often used short-term or as prep for procedures.

Myomectomy (Fibroid Removal)

Removes fibroids while preserving the uterus—via hysteroscopy, laparoscopy, or open surgery—ideal when you wish to conceive.

Hysterectomy

Definitive treatment for large, multiple, or recurrent fibroids when family is complete; eliminates bleeding and pressure symptoms.

Uterine Fibroid Embolization (UFE)

Minimally invasive; blocks fibroid blood supply so they shrink naturally. Day-care approach with quicker recovery, uterus preserved.

Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA)

Targets and shrinks fibroids with heat through tiny probes; precise, minimal scarring, and rapid symptom relief.

Diagnosis & Imaging

Pelvic exam, ultrasound, MRI, and hysteroscopy define size & location—so we can choose the most effective, least invasive option.
