Blocked Fallopian Tubes

Blocked fallopian tubes are one of the leading causes of female infertility in Pune. The fallopian tubes play a critical role in natural conception by transporting eggs from the ovaries to the uterus for fertilisation. Any obstruction prevents sperm or the fertilised egg from reaching the uterus, leading to difficulty conceiving. At Nakshatra Clinic in Pune, we provide advanced diagnosis, personalised treatment, and expert fertility care to help women overcome tubal infertility and achieve their dreams of motherhood.

Our Specialized Treatment Options for Blocked Fallopian Tubes

Surgical Intervention

Surgery is recommended for women with partial tubal blockages. In Pune, minimally invasive laparoscopic surgery is preferred, allowing the removal of scar tissue or adhesions and creating a clear pathway for the egg. Recovery is faster, incisions are small, and fertility chances improve significantly under expert care.

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

Facing repeated IVF failures? We specialize in advanced diagnostics, uterine evaluation, and personalized embryo transfer protocols to improve implantation success rates naturally.

Therapeutic Hysterosalpingography (HSG)

HSG is a diagnostic X-ray procedure using contrast dye to check tubal patency. In some cases, the dye itself can help flush minor blockages, improving fertility naturally. This non-invasive procedure is commonly performed in Pune and provides both diagnostic and therapeutic benefits for women facing mild tubal obstruction.

Laparoscopic Tuboplasty

Laparoscopic tuboplasty is a minimally invasive surgery to repair blocked fallopian tubes. Small incisions and advanced cameras allow precise removal of adhesions or reconstructing damaged tubes. This approach reduces recovery time, minimizes pain, and significantly improves the chances of natural conception for women in Pune struggling with tubal infertility.
