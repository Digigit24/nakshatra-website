Egg & Sperm Freezing

Egg and sperm freezing, scientifically known as oocyte cryopreservation and sperm cryopreservation, are among the most reliable ways to preserve your fertility for the future. These treatments are ideal for anyone who wishes to delay parenthood, prevent age-related fertility decline, or safeguard reproductive potential before medical treatments.

Fertility preservation is not just a medical process; it is an empowering decision that gives you complete control over your future family planning. Whether you’re an individual preparing for a demanding career, a couple wanting to build financial stability first, or someone undergoing a health treatment that may impact fertility, freezing eggs or sperm ensures your options remain open—without pressure or compromise.

Egg & Sperm Freezing Services We Provide

Complete Fertility Evaluation & Counselling

We begin with a thorough fertility assessment, including ovarian reserve testing, hormone analysis, semen testing, and ultrasound scans. This helps understand reproductive health and choose the right time for freezing. Our counselling explains every step in simple words so patients feel prepared, confident, and fully supported throughout their fertility preservation journey.

Advanced Egg Retrieval & Vitrification

Egg retrieval is performed safely under ultrasound guidance to collect healthy eggs. These eggs are immediately frozen using vitrification, a rapid cooling method that prevents damage and preserves quality. This advanced technique ensures long-term egg survival, offering women strong pregnancy chances whenever they decide to start a family in the future.

High-Quality Sperm Collection & Cryopreservation

Sperm samples are collected, processed, and frozen using international cryopreservation standards. Even men with low sperm count can often store viable samples. The process is quick, comfortable, and ideal for future IVF or ICSI treatments. Cryopreserved sperm remains stable for years, giving men complete flexibility to plan parenthood later.

Future IVF Planning & Embryo Creation

When ready for pregnancy, frozen eggs or sperm can be used for IVF, ICSI, or embryo transfer. Our team guides patients through every step—from thawing to fertilization and embryo growth. This option helps couples and individuals achieve successful conception using preserved reproductive cells at the ideal time in life.

Safe, Secure Long-Term Storage

Frozen eggs and sperm are stored in advanced cryogenic tanks with consistent ultra-low temperatures monitored round the clock. Each sample is labelled and secured to maintain identity and safety. This reliable long-term storage ensures reproductive cells remain healthy and ready for future IVF use, providing absolute confidence and peace of mind.

Fertility Preservation for Medical Treatments

People undergoing chemotherapy, radiation, or surgeries often freeze eggs or sperm to protect future fertility. We offer urgent appointments and quick processing to ensure nothing is delayed. This service provides emotional reassurance and safeguards reproductive potential, helping patients focus on treatment while keeping their dream of future parenthood safe.

Benefits of Egg & Sperm Freezing

Preserves Fertility for the Future

Egg and sperm freezing allow individuals and couples to secure their ability to conceive later when the time is right. It offers peace of mind for those prioritizing career, education, or personal health. By freezing younger and healthier reproductive cells, chances of a successful pregnancy are significantly improved.

Helps Individuals Facing Medical Treatments

Cancer therapy, chemotherapy, radiation, or major surgery can affect fertility. Freezing eggs or sperm before treatment helps protect reproductive potential. This gives patients emotional and reproductive security, ensuring they still have the opportunity for biological children once treatment and recovery are completed.

Useful for Women With Declining Ovarian Reserve

As a woman ages, egg quality and quantity decrease. Egg freezing preserves eggs when they are younger and healthier, improving success rates in future IVF cycles. It is especially beneficial for women with PCOS, endometriosis, or premature ovarian failure risk, empowering them with more control over family planning.

Helps Couples with IVF Planning Flexibility

Freezing sperm and eggs allows couples to plan IVF cycles without time pressure. It ensures availability of high-quality genetic material, even if one partner is unavailable or biologically stressed during treatment. This increases embryo success rates and supports smooth, well-timed fertility procedures.

Supports Family Planning for LGBTQ+ Individuals

Egg and sperm freezing is a valuable option for LGBTQ+ couples planning future parenthood. It allows them to preserve biological fertility before transitioning or medical treatment. With stored gametes, they can pursue surrogacy, donor IVF, or assisted conception when they are ready to grow their family.

Higher Success Rates in Assisted Reproductive Treatments

Frozen eggs and sperm, when stored properly, retain excellent quality and work effectively in IVF. Techniques like vitrification ensure high survival rates after thawing. This increases chances of fertilization, embryo formation, and healthy pregnancy outcomes, supporting better IVF success rates.
