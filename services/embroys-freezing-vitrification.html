Embryos Freezing & Vitrification...

Embryo freezing, also known as cryopreservation, is an advanced fertility preservation technique offered at our fertility clinic in Pune. It involves cooling and storing embryos at ultra-low temperatures for future use, helping couples plan pregnancy later. At Nakshatra Clinic, our fertility specialists use both slow freezing and the modern vitrification method to preserve embryo quality with precision and safety. This process is ideal for couples undergoing IVF treatment in Pune, women choosing to delay pregnancy, or those undergoing medical treatments that may impact fertility. Our clinic in Pune ensures the highest embryo survival rates through advanced embryo freezing and vitrification techniques, providing patients with reliable fertility preservation options for the future.

Our Embryo Freezing & Vitrification Treatment

1. Detailed Fertility Evaluation

Before freezing, our doctors assess egg and sperm quality through a complete fertility check. This helps identify the most viable embryos for freezing and ensures the best possible outcome during future fertility treatment.

2. IVF and Embryo Formation

Embryos are created using the IVF technique, where eggs are fertilized with selected sperm in a controlled lab environment. The healthiest embryos are chosen for freezing after careful monitoring by our embryology team.

3. Advanced Vitrification Technique

We use a rapid-freezing process called vitrification, which prevents ice crystal formation and preserves embryo structure. This technique increases embryo survival rates and helps achieve successful pregnancies when embryos are thawed later.

4. Safe Embryo Storage

All embryos are stored in ultra-low temperatures using advanced cryopreservation systems. Continuous temperature monitoring ensures embryos remain stable and safe for extended periods without losing their biological quality

5. Embryo Thawing and Transfer

When you’re ready for pregnancy, our team carefully thaws the preserved embryos and transfers them into the uterus at the optimal time. This process ensures the highest chance of implantation and healthy pregnancy outcomes.

6. Fertility Preservation for Medical Reasons

Embryo freezing is ideal for individuals undergoing treatments like chemotherapy or surgery that may affect fertility. It allows you to preserve embryos safely before starting medical procedures, keeping future family options open.

7. Flexible Family Planning

For couples wishing to delay parenthood, embryo freezing provides the freedom to conceive later. This option supports both personal and professional goals without compromising fertility potential.

8. High Success and Survival Rates

Our vitrification process has a high embryo survival rate after thawing. Studies show frozen embryos can remain viable for many years, giving couples an excellent chance of achieving pregnancy through IVF.

9. Personalized Guidance and Support

From consultation to embryo transfer, our fertility experts guide you through every stage. We provide transparent information, emotional support, and a personalized plan to make your parenthood journey smooth and confident.
