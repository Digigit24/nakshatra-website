Female Infertility Surgeries

At Nakshatra Clinic, Pune , we understand that surgical intervention can be a crucial step in overcoming female infertility challenges . Our team of highly skilled surgeons specializes in minimally invasive and advanced surgical procedures to address various reproductive health issues. With state-of-the-art technology and patient-centered care , we provide the highest quality surgical treatments in Pune.

Whether you're dealing with uterine abnormalities, tubal blockages, or ovarian issues , our clinic in Pune offers personalized surgical solutions that maximize fertility potential while ensuring minimal recovery time and optimal outcomes . Our goal is to make fertility surgery accessible, safe, and effective for every woman seeking help in the Pune region.

Surgical Solutions for Female Infertility at Nakshatra Clinic

Hysteroscopic Surgery

Our experienced surgeons perform hysteroscopic procedures to diagnose and treat uterine abnormalities such as polyps, fibroids, adhesions, and septums. This minimally invasive approach allows for precise correction of intrauterine issues that may be preventing pregnancy, with faster recovery and minimal discomfort.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Our specialists utilize advanced laparoscopic techniques to treat conditions like endometriosis, ovarian cysts, tubal blockages, and pelvic adhesions. This keyhole surgery approach offers significant benefits including smaller incisions, reduced pain, shorter hospital stays, and quicker return to normal activities.

Tubal Surgeries

For women with tubal factor infertility , we offer various surgical options including tubal reanastomosis (reversal of tubal ligation), salpingostomy (creating new tubal opening), and salpingectomy (removal of damaged tubes). Our surgeons carefully evaluate each case to determine the most appropriate procedure for optimal results.

Ovarian Drilling

For women with PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) who don't respond to medication, ovarian drilling can be an effective treatment. This laparoscopic procedure involves making small punctures in the ovary to reduce androgen production and restore ovulation in many cases.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Recovery time varies depending on the procedure. Minimally invasive surgeries like hysteroscopy and laparoscopy typically require 1-2 weeks for full recovery, while more complex procedures may need 4-6 weeks. Our team provides detailed post-operative care instructions and follow-up to ensure optimal healing.

Most fertility surgeries are performed under general anesthesia to ensure patient comfort and safety. Our experienced anesthesiologists specialize in reproductive surgeries and work closely with the surgical team to provide the safest possible experience.

The timing depends on the specific procedure performed. For some surgeries like hysteroscopic polyp removal, you may be able to try conceiving in the next cycle. For more complex procedures, we typically recommend waiting 2-3 months to allow complete healing. Your surgeon will provide personalized guidance based on your specific situation.

Success rates vary depending on the specific condition being treated, the patient's age, and other fertility factors. For example, hysteroscopic removal of uterine polyps or septums can significantly improve pregnancy rates. Our team will discuss realistic expectations and success rates specific to your condition during your consultation.

In some cases, non-surgical treatments may be available depending on the specific condition. However, for structural issues like uterine abnormalities, tubal blockages, or severe endometriosis, surgery often provides the most direct and effective solution. Our specialists will discuss all available options, both surgical and non-surgical, to help you make an informed decision.
