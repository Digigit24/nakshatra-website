Hysteroscopy

Hysteroscopy is a minimally invasive diagnostic and therapeutic procedure used to examine the inside of the uterus with a thin, lighted instrument called a hysteroscope. This advanced gynaecological technique helps specialists visualise the uterine cavity, endometrium, and cervical canal to identify and treat problems like abnormal uterine bleeding, fibroids, or polyps.

At our advanced fertility and gynaecology centre in Pune, experienced specialists perform both diagnostic hysteroscopy and operative hysteroscopy with precision and care.

Conditions Treated with Hysteroscopy

1. Abnormal Uterine Bleeding (AUB)

Evaluates heavy or irregular periods and enables targeted treatment of fibroids, polyps, or lining issues—improving cycles and fertility outcomes in Pune.

2. Endometrial Polyps

Removes small growths that cause spotting or infertility—quick relief with no major surgery and better chances of conception.

3. Uterine Fibroids

Minimally invasive removal of submucosal fibroids to ease heavy bleeding, pain, and infertility—helping restore comfort and reproductive health.

4. Intrauterine Adhesions (Asherman’s)

Gently releases scar tissue to restore the uterine cavity—improving periods and fertility after surgery or infection.

5. Uterine Septum / Structural Issues

Corrects congenital septum and cavity irregularities—reducing miscarriage risk and creating a healthy site for implantation.

6. Retained Products of Conception (RPOC)

Safely removes retained tissue after miscarriage or delivery—preventing infection, bleeding, and future complications.

7. Foreign Bodies / IUD Complications

Locates and removes misplaced or embedded IUDs precisely—reducing pain, preventing infection, and preserving fertility.

8. Endometrial Growths / Thickening

Allows direct biopsy and treatment of abnormal lining changes—supporting early detection and uterine health.

9. Postmenopausal Bleeding

Visualises the cavity to diagnose polyps, hyperplasia, or cancer—enabling timely, accurate treatment in Pune.

10. Infertility Evaluation

Detects hidden uterine factors (polyps, adhesions, septum) that affect implantation—boosting natural and IVF success rates.

Types of Hysteroscopy Procedures

1. Office Hysteroscopy

A quick, outpatient evaluation for endometrial polyps, uterine septum, or irregular bleeding.

  • Quick recovery
  • Minimal discomfort
  • No hospital stay
2. Operative Hysteroscopy

Performed under anesthesia for definitive, uterus-sparing treatment.

  • Removes fibroids and polyps
  • Treats intrauterine adhesions (Asherman’s)
  • Corrects congenital uterine anomalies
