Advanced IVF Treatments at Nakshatra Clinic

Advanced IVF Treatment

Our experienced doctors provide advanced IVF treatments, including ICSI, blastocyst culture, and laser-assisted hatching, to help couples overcome fertility challenges safely and effectively, increasing the chances of successful conception.

Donor Services

Qualified specialists assist with donor programs, including egg, sperm, and embryo donation, ensuring ethical, safe, and confidential procedures to support individuals and couples who need extra help in their fertility journey.

Advanced Tests

Our team conducts comprehensive fertility tests, such as ERA, PGT, and detailed male and female assessments, to identify the root causes of infertility and design a precise, personalized treatment plan for each patient.

Freezing Services

Doctors at our clinic offer embryo and egg freezing (vitrification), preserving fertility for future IVF cycles while maintaining high success rates and safety, giving patients peace of mind and flexibility in planning parenthood.
