Laparoscopy

Laparoscopy is a modern, minimally invasive surgical technique used to diagnose and treat gynaecological and abdominal conditions. At Nakshatra Clinic, our experienced gynaecologists perform advanced laparoscopic surgery for women—including treatment for fibroids, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, and infertility. Using small incisions and a high-definition laparoscope, we ensure safe, precise, and comfortable procedures with faster recovery, minimal scarring, and improved reproductive outcomes in Pune.

Procedure Details of Laparoscopic Surgery

1. Anaesthesia

Usually performed under general anaesthesia to keep you pain-free. Minor procedures may use local anaesthesia—your Pune surgeon will choose what’s safest for you.

2. Incisions

Tiny cuts (typically 1–4 mm) near the navel/lower abdomen allow the scope and instruments—meaning minimal scarring and quicker recovery.

3. Carbon Dioxide Gas Inflation

CO₂ gently expands the abdomen to create working space, helping your surgeon see clearly and operate precisely.

4. Insertion of Laparoscope

A thin, illuminated camera provides a real-time, high-definition view of pelvic organs for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

5. Use of Additional Instruments

Through additional micro-incisions, we can remove fibroids/cysts/adhesions or take biopsies with minimal trauma.

6. Tissue Sampling (Biopsy)

If needed, small samples are collected to check for infections, abnormal cells, or other causes of pain/bleeding/infertility.

7. Closing the Incisions

Instruments are removed and cuts are closed with sutures/tape. Tiny incisions = lower infection risk and barely-visible scars.

8. Post-Procedure Observation

Most patients go home the same day; complex surgeries may need an overnight stay for comfort and safety.

Why Laparoscopy is Performed

Infertility Evaluation

Detects blocked tubes, endometriosis, or adhesions to plan effective conception care.

Diagnosis of Pelvic Pain

Helps find endometriosis, cysts, or adhesions when scans are inconclusive.

Fibroid & Cyst Treatment

Removes fibroids/cysts laparoscopically to preserve the uterus and fertility.

Biopsy / Tissue Sampling

Checks infection, abnormal cells, or tumours for accurate diagnosis.

Adhesion / Scar Removal

Frees organs from scar tissue to reduce pain and improve fertility.

Minimally Invasive Benefits

Smaller cuts, shorter stay, faster recovery, and minimal pain.
