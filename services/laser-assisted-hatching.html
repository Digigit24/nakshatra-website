Laser-Assisted Hatching

Every couple dreams of holding their little miracle — and we’re here to help make that dream come true. Our advanced Laser Assisted Hatching (LAH) services are designed to improve embryo implantation and enhance IVF success rates safely and precisely. This gentle fertility technique is especially beneficial for those who’ve experienced previous IVF failures or face unique fertility challenges. At Nakshatra Clinic, we blend cutting-edge technology with compassionate care to bring you closer to the joy of parenthood.

Laser-Assisted Hatching at Nakshatra Clinic

1. Embryo Assessment & Preparation

Our fertility experts perform a detailed embryo assessment before Laser Assisted Hatching. This helps identify embryos that may benefit most from the procedure, ensuring higher IVF success rates and improving the chances of successful embryo implantation in the uterus.

2. Precision Laser Opening Creation

Using advanced infrared laser technology, a small and precise hole is created in the embryo’s outer shell (zona pellucida). This gentle fertility technique allows the embryo to hatch easily, promoting better implantation and enhancing the overall outcome of IVF treatment in Pune.

3. Safe Embryo Handling

At Nakshatra Clinic, every IVF embryo is handled with utmost safety using modern fertility lab techniques. The laser-assisted hatching process takes only seconds and ensures embryo protection, making it a safe and reliable method to boost IVF pregnancy success.

4. Embryo Transfer & Post-Procedure Care

After completing Laser Assisted Hatching, the embryo is carefully transferred into the uterus under expert guidance. Our clinic provides complete post-IVF monitoring and fertility support, ensuring that the embryo develops in a healthy and nurturing environment.

5. Advanced IVF Laboratory Facilities

Nakshatra Clinic features a state-of-the-art IVF laboratory equipped with modern laser systems, high-resolution microscopes, and temperature-controlled incubators. These facilities help ensure accurate hatching, safe embryo handling, and improved fertility treatment outcomes.

6. Personalized Treatment Plans

Every couple receives a customized IVF treatment plan based on fertility history, age, and embryo quality. This personalized care approach helps optimize laser-assisted hatching results and enhances the overall pregnancy success rate.

7. High Success Rate & Support

Nakshatra Clinic is known for its high IVF success rates and compassionate patient care. Our experienced fertility specialists use the latest assisted reproductive technologies to maximize implantation chances and provide complete emotional and medical support throughout the fertility journey.

8. Safe, Quick & Painless

The Laser Assisted Hatching procedure is a safe, quick, and painless fertility treatment. It minimizes handling, reduces embryo stress, and ensures a higher possibility of implantation—making it a preferred option for couples seeking advanced IVF treatments in Pune.
