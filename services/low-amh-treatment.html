Low AMH Treatment

Being told you have low AMH levels can feel overwhelming. But here’s the truth: low AMH doesn’t mean your dream of motherhood is impossible. It simply indicates that your ovarian reserve may be lower than average, and you might benefit from a personalised fertility approach.

At Nakshatra Clinic, we provide low AMH treatment in Pune for women struggling with diminished ovarian reserve. Our experienced fertility specialists offer advanced diagnostics, IVF for low AMH, and supportive, patient-centred care to help you take confident steps toward parenthood.

Our Personalized Low AMH Treatment

Advanced Fertility Testing

We begin with a complete evaluation, including AMH blood tests, Antral Follicle Count (AFC), and hormone profiling. These tests help our specialists understand your ovarian reserve and design a treatment plan tailored to your unique fertility needs.

Improving Egg Quality

Through a combination of lifestyle guidance, targeted supplements like CoQ10 and DHEA, and nutritional support, we help improve your egg quality naturally. Small changes in daily habits can make a big difference in boosting your fertility potential.

IVF and ICSI for Low AMH

For women with low AMH, we offer gentle IVF protocols and ICSI to optimize fertilization. Our expert team carefully monitors each cycle, ensuring the best possible outcomes while keeping your comfort and safety a priority.

Advanced Treatments

We provide advanced options such as Ovarian PRP Therapy to rejuvenate ovarian function and egg or embryo freezing for future planning. These treatments are designed to give women with low AMH more opportunities to conceive.
