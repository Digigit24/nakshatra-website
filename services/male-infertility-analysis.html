Male Infertility Analysis

Infertility affects both partners, and in nearly 40% of couples, the main cause is male infertility. At our male infertility clinic in Pune, we offer a comprehensive male fertility evaluation to identify the exact reason for difficulty in conceiving. Our specialists perform advanced semen analysis, hormonal testing, and sperm motility and morphology assessments to determine the root cause. Depending on your diagnosis, we provide effective male infertility treatment in Pune, including lifestyle modification programs, medications to improve sperm health, and advanced assisted reproductive techniques such as IUI (Intrauterine Insemination), IVF (In Vitro Fertilization), and ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection). With personalized care and modern technology, we help couples achieve successful conception and overcome low sperm count, poor sperm motility, or other male reproductive issues.

Our 3-Step Male Infertility Evaluation Process

1. Detailed Medical History

We begin with a thorough review of your health and lifestyle to identify potential causes.

  • Reproductive and sexual history
  • Past surgeries, injuries, or infections
  • Medication or steroid use
  • Family medical background
  • Smoking, alcohol, or occupational exposures

2. Semen Analysis

The first and most crucial diagnostic step is semen analysis.

  • Sperm count and concentration
  • Motility (movement)
  • Morphology (shape)
  • Volume, pH, and liquefaction time

Samples are examined under high magnification using advanced laboratory techniques. For accurate results, patients are advised 2–5 days of abstinence before testing. If abnormalities are found, repeat testing is conducted for confirmation.

3. Specialized Fertility Tests

If the initial findings indicate deeper issues, we offer specialized tests, including:

  • Sperm DNA Fragmentation Index (DFI) – assesses sperm DNA integrity
  • Karyotyping – identifies chromosomal abnormalities
  • Y-Chromosome Microdeletion Test – detects genetic defects affecting sperm production
  • Cystic Fibrosis Gene Mutation Test – checks for genetic conditions linked to infertility

These tests help identify unexplained infertility and guide treatment for complex cases such as recurrent IVF failure or severe oligospermia.

Treatment Options After Diagnosis

Based on the test results, our fertility specialists create a customized plan which may include.

  • Lifestyle and dietary modifications to improve sperm quality
  • Medications or hormonal therapy to correct imbalances
  • Surgical treatments such as PESA, TESE, or Micro-TESE for sperm retrieval in azoospermia cases
  • Assisted Reproductive Techniques (ART) such as IUI, IVF, or ICSI to help achieve pregnancy

Our approach focuses on personalized, evidence-based male infertility treatment to optimize conception outcomes.
