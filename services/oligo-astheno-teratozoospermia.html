Oligo Astheno Teratozoospermia...

Oligo-Astheno-Teratozoospermia Infertility is a common concern affecting couples worldwide, and one of the main causes is low sperm count and poor motility. Nearly 90% of male infertility cases are linked to sperm quality issues, and 1 in 6 men faces challenges in conceiving.
At Nakshatra Clinic in Pune, we focus on identifying and treating problems like low sperm count, poor sperm movement, and abnormal sperm shape, which make it hard to conceive naturally. With personalized care, advanced treatments, and expert guidance, most men in Pune and surrounding areas can improve fertility and achieve successful conception.

Oligo Astheno Teratozoospermia (OAT) Treatment

Varicocele Surgery (Varicocelctomy)

This procedure corrects enlarged scrotal veins to improve sperm count and motility . It enhances blood flow, reduces discomfort, and supports natural conception — a safe and effective option for infertility caused by varicocele.

Hormonal & Medical Therapy

Customized hormonal and medical treatments help restore balance, boost testosterone, and improve sperm quality. Doctor-guided medications and antioxidants support natural improvement in sperm count and motility.

Percutaneous Embolization

A minimally invasive way to correct varicocele by blocking problematic veins. It improves testicular temperature and sperm production with faster recovery and minimal pain , helping enhance fertility outcomes.

Sperm Retrieval Procedures (TESA, PESA, Micro-TESE)

Advanced microsurgical techniques like TESA, PESA, and Micro-TESE retrieve healthy sperm directly from the testicles or epididymis. These support fertility treatments such as IVF and ICSI for men with very low or absent sperm in semen.

Assisted Reproductive Techniques (IVF, ICSI, IUI)

Modern ART options increase the chances of conception. IVF combines eggs and sperm in the lab, ICSI injects a single sperm into an egg, and IUI places prepared sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation to enhance pregnancy rates.

Lifestyle & Fertility Counseling

Personalised fertility counseling helps men improve sperm quality through better diet, exercise, sleep, and stress control. Reducing smoking, alcohol, and heat exposure supports long-term reproductive wellness and fertility success.
