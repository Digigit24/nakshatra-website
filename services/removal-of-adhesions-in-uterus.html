Removal of Adhesions

Uterine adhesions, also known as Asherman’s Syndrome, can affect a woman’s menstrual health and fertility. At our Pune-based fertility and gynaecology centre, our experienced doctors and laparoscopic specialists provide safe, minimally invasive adhesion removal treatments to restore uterine function, improve fertility, and relieve pelvic discomfort. Our advanced diagnostic techniques and compassionate care ensure every woman receives personalised, effective, and long-lasting results.

Treatment Options for Removal of Adhesions

Hysteroscopic Adhesiolysis (Minimally Invasive Surgery)

This is the most common and effective treatment for Asherman’s Syndrome. During this procedure, a hysteroscope is inserted into the uterus, and tiny surgical instruments or lasers are used to cut and remove scar tissues precisely without damaging healthy tissue. This procedure is performed under anesthesia and has a high success rate in restoring fertility.

Hormonal Therapy (Estrogen Support)

After surgical removal, estrogen therapy is prescribed to stimulate the regrowth of the endometrial lining. It also helps prevent new scar formation and regulates menstrual cycles.

Post-Surgery Care & Prevention

To prevent recurrence of adhesions:

  • A uterine stent or balloon may be temporarily placed inside the uterus.
  • Regular follow-up hysteroscopies are done to monitor healing.
  • Antibiotics and hormonal therapy ensure complete recovery.
