TESA & Sperm Retrieval

At Nakshatra Clinic Pune, we offer Testicular Sperm Aspiration (TESA) — a minimally invasive procedure to retrieve sperm directly from the testicles. This advanced fertility technique helps men with azoospermia or low sperm count achieve fatherhood through IVF or ICSI. Our skilled urologists and fertility experts in Pune ensure a safe, effective, and personalized TESA procedure, combining medical expertise with compassionate care to support your journey toward parenthood.

Sperm Retrieval Services

TESA – Testicular Sperm Aspiration

TESA is a minimally invasive sperm retrieval method where a fine needle extracts sperm directly from the testicle. Performed under local anesthesia, it’s ideal for men with azoospermia or duct blockages causing infertility.

PESA – Percutaneous Epididymal Sperm Aspiration

PESA is a simple clinic-based sperm retrieval technique performed under local anesthesia. It’s highly effective for men with obstructive azoospermia caused by vasectomy, infection, or blocked reproductive ducts, ensuring minimal discomfort and fast recovery.

TESE – Testicular Sperm Extraction

TESE involves making a small incision in the testicle to collect sperm from seminiferous tubules. This method is suitable for men with non-obstructive azoospermia and is performed safely under sedation or anesthesia in an operating room.

MESA – Microepididymal Sperm Aspiration

MESA uses a high-powered surgical microscope to retrieve sperm from the epididymis under general anesthesia. It’s ideal for men with vasal or epididymal obstruction and offers superior sperm quality for IVF or ICSI procedures.

Microdissection TESE (Micro-TESE)

Microdissection TESE is an advanced, microscope-guided sperm retrieval technique that helps men with low or zero sperm production. It offers higher sperm recovery rates and minimal tissue damage, making it the preferred treatment for severe azoospermia.

Non-Surgical: Penile Vibratory Stimulation (PVS)

PVS is a gentle, non-invasive technique that uses a medical-grade vibrator placed at the tip of the penis to stimulate ejaculation. It helps men with spinal injuries or anejaculation naturally release semen for fertility purposes.

Non-Surgical: Electro-Ejaculation (EEJ)

EEJ uses mild electrical stimulation through a special probe to trigger ejaculation when natural methods aren’t possible. Performed under medical supervision, it’s safe and effective for men with retrograde ejaculation or nerve-related fertility issues.
